HOLY MACKEREL!

Yello, I'm Rango LF, a first year in the Department of Mechanical and Industrial Engineering at the University of Toronto.

I created this website for a few different reasons. One, as a semi-professional alternative to my personal website hosted on Neocities. I'd like to present a more polished version of myself and my interests, academic journey, and professional experience to fellow peers, colleagues, friends, family members, and the like.

Second, I want a space to share the advice and insights I’ve gained while navigating this program. Before and during my time here, I was fortunate to receive valuable guidance from kind upper-years (special thanks to Hassan T.!) during moments of doubt. I hope to pay that kindness forward by supporting the future frosh who come after me.

And finally, I want something to look back on when I'm old and wrinkly at 30, maybe. I spent a lot of my life thinking I'd never make it to adulthood, least of all university, so I want to record and treasure these 4-5 years. Thank you for being here. I hope you enjoy your stay :)
