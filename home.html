Yello, I'm Rango LF, a first year in the Department of Mechanical and Industrial Engineering at the University of Toronto.

I created this website for a few different reasons. One, as a semi-professional alternative to my much more personal website hosted on Neocities. I'd like to present a more polished version of myself and my interests, academic journey, and professional experience to fellow peers, colleagues, friends, family members, and the like.

Second, I wanted a place to share my advice and experiences with prospective, incoming, and junior students as I progress through the program. Before and during my time here, I received valuable guidance from kind upper-years (shout out to Hassan T.!) in my moments of doubt. I hope to repay that generosity by helping the Frosh who'll come after me.

And finally, I want something to look back on when I'm old and wrinkly at 30, maybe. I spent a lot of my life thinking I'd never make it to adulthood, least of all university, so I want to record and treasure these 4-5 years.

Thank you for being here. I hope you enjoy your stay :)
